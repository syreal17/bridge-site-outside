# bridge dev log

Bridge is about walking with someone between two worlds until both of you
change. The player assumes the role of a hiker who stumbles into an
otherworldly being in their dreams and then in the woods. You grow closer
through quiet walks and conversations over the campfire. What is Faerie, and
what does it mean that it draws inexorably closer?
